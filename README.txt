1. Just run pong.java and press SPACE to start the game.
2. You have three lives throughout the game and 10 seconds for each level.
3. Every time the paddle hits the ball you get a point.
4. As you level up, the paddle shortens and the ball speeds up.
5. You can use both direction keys and mouse pointer to control the paddle.
6. Feature: Every time you hit the ball, there is an increase of random upward velocity to simulate the real life bouncing movement. Also, when you move the paddle to left/right, the ball moves accordingly.
7. Feature: When you hit the bonus box, you get a random number of bonus points.

ENJOY!
